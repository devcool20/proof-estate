India has over 4.9 million hectares of land under active legal dispute. The current verification process for a commercial property involves a title search at the sub-registrar's office (15–20 days), an encumbrance certificate check (5–7 days), a CERSAI/NeSL charge search (3–5 days), RERA compliance verification (2–3 days), and a legal opinion from a qualified advocate — totaling 30–45 days and costing ₹50,000–₹2,00,000. The process is manual, non-standardized, and still unreliable. A platform that automates even 60% of this process creates an immediate, quantifiable value proposition: 48-hour turnaround at a fraction of the cost.
